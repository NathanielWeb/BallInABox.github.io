Welcome to Ball in a Box!!

In this game you can earn almost triple the amount of tickets you spend!

Here is how the game works

  • In this game, there will be 5 boxes to choose from. In each box lies two balls, a green one and a red one. Each ball has a number and at the end, the sum of the numbers will represent how many tickets you earn.
  • The first step is to choose how many tickets you want to spend. The minimum is 7 tickets which can earn you up to 20 tickets. If you spend 14 tickets, then you will be able to earn up to 40 tickets. 21 tickets can give you up to 60 tickets.
  • The second step is to pick one of the 5 boxes that are displayed to you. Each box has a different pair of balls in them.
  • Next, the balls from the box that you picked will be displayed to you. The program will calculate the sum and tell you how many tickets you earned in total.
  • The program will also calculate your net earnings and show whether you made a profit or a loss.
  • Finally, you will have the option to play again.
